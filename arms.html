Technical SEO Engineer (Remote)

BrightForge Labs is a growing digital technology company that builds and optimizes high-traffic websites for SaaS startups, publishers, and global e-commerce brands. We are looking for a Technical SEO Engineer who understands how search engines crawl, render, and index large-scale websites.

Key Responsibilities

  • Monitor crawling and indexing behavior in Google Search Console
  • Manage XML sitemaps and discovery workflows
  • Fix technical issues affecting indexation
  • Analyze server logs and Googlebot activity
  • Work closely with developers on performance improvements

Requirements

  • 2+ years experience in Technical SEO
  • Understanding of HTTP, redirects, canonical tags
  • Experience with large websites preferred
  • Basic scripting knowledge is a plus

Benefits

  • Remote work
  • Flexible schedule
  • Competitive monthly salary
  • Long-term growth opportunities
